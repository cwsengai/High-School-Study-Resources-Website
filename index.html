About Us

Hello! My name is Charlotte, and I am a second-year student at the University of Toronto, specializing in both Computer Science and Data Science. I created this website to share my academic journey and offer valuable study resources that have helped me succeed.

Before university, I attended St. Robert Catholic High School, where I graduated with a 99.2% average. During high school, I actively participated in various Waterloo mathematics and computing competitions, earning the title of School Champion and achieving a Perfect Score in the Galois Contest.

Having more than a year of tutoring experience, my goal is to empower other students with the tools and support they need to thrive academically. I hope you find this site both helpful and inspiring!
